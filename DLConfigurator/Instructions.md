1. Download the repository
2. If on linux, open the linux folder and open the index.html in the browser of your choice. 
2a. If on windows, open the install.bat and then open the site via launch.bat
3. Customize however you like and click the green **Download gameinfo.gi** button and replace your gameinfo.gi.
